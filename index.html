Quality of Life Improvements & New Features

Quality of Life (QoL)

1
Auto-Skip Dialogues
Automatically skip repetitive NPC dialogues when starting missions, entering trials, or interacting with frequently visited NPCs.
2
Display Real Spin Chances
Display real spin chances with the player's active Lucky bonuses already applied to show accurate probabilities.
3
Map Star Progress Display
Show the number of stars unlocked or completed in each map to track overall progress more easily.
4
Missing Sound Effects
Add missing sound effects for talent spins, NPC kills, item drops, and the auto-collect feature to enhance game feedback.
5
Protection for Mythic & Shiny Characters
Prevent accidental deletion or auto-selling of Mythic or Shiny characters with a protection system.
6
Advanced Inventory Filters
Filter characters by strength, level, rarity, DPS, and other attributes for better inventory management.
7
Search by Passive
Allow players to search characters by their passive abilities in the inventory for easier team building.
8
Confirmation for High-Rarity Rolls
Show confirmation warnings before rerolling Mythic or Legendary passives and talents to prevent costly mistakes.
9
Server Version Display
Display the current server version on the game screen and list fixes for each version in patch notes.
10
DPS Leaderboard Reset After Balance Changes
Automatically reset the DPS leaderboard after balance changes to ensure fair competition with updated mechanics.
11
Damage Test Dummy
Add a training dummy where players can freely test DPS, skills, passives, and builds without wasting resources.
12
Drop History Log
Display a short history of recent drops during fast farming or AFK gameplay so players don't miss important items.
13
AFK Indicator
Clearly indicate when a player is AFK in co-op content, trials, and dungeons to improve team coordination.

Features

1
Guild System
Add guilds with guild bosses and a guild leaderboard to encourage teamwork and create a stronger community.
2
Weekend Event Voting
Allow players to vote for which event will be active during the weekend (Lucky, Drop Rate, EXP, etc.).
3
Accessory Forging System
Forge accessories using parts from towers, dungeons, or trials through a puzzle-solving or crafting mechanic.
4
Daily Missions System
Add daily missions with difficulty scaling based on player progression to encourage regular engagement.
5
Top Global Chat Indicator
Display a Top Global player's leaderboard rank and category next to their name in chat for recognition.
6
Auto Attack Range Upgrade
Increase auto-attack range through upgrades available in the Trial Shop for improved farming efficiency.
7
Sprint Upgrade (Trial Only)
Purchase a sprint upgrade in the Trial Shop that grants extra movement speed only during trials.
8
Inventory Expansion by Map Completion
Grant additional inventory slots after collecting all characters from a map (Common, Rare, Epic, etc.) as a completion reward.
9
Free Mounts System
Add free mounts obtainable through gameplay, since most mounts are currently premium-only content.
10
Private Server Kick Menu
Add a Kick Menu for private server owners to remove players from the server, helping maintain control over their private sessions.
